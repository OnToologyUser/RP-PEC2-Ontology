ExcellentReviewback to ToC

Rule which infers that the type of Review made by a Passenger is Excellent if its overall mark is >9.

Passenger c(?P) , Review c(?R) , HasCreatedReview op(?P,?R) , OverallRating dp(?R,?v) , greaterThanOrEqual(?) -> excellent review c(?R)

NegativeReviewback to ToC

Rule which infers that the type of Review made by a Passenger is Negative if its overall mark is < 5.

Passenger c(?P) , Review c(?R) , HasCreatedReview op(?P,?R) , OverallRating dp(?R,?v) , lessThan(?) -> NegativeReview c(?R)

PositiveReviewback to ToC

Rule which infers that the type of Review made by a Passenger is Positive if its overall mark is >=5 and <9.

Passenger c(?P) , Review c(?R) , HasCreatedReview op(?P,?R) , OverallRating dp(?R,?v) , greaterThanOrEqual(?) , lessThan(?) -> PositiveReview c(?R)
